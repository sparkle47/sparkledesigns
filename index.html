ABOUT US


WE ARE YOUR MOST AUDACIOUS VISION:

Sparkle designs is a creative design studio based in Nairobi,Kenya. we an experienced and passionate group of designers and developers. Every client we work with becomes a part of the team. Together we face the challenges and celebrate the success tales. Best-in-class creative projects require technology that perfectly supports the design. We rely on the right tools for the job, not a one-size-fits-all tech stack. We keep the lines of communication wide open and reciprocal.
